Classic Burger

Description

I have loved burgers for as long as I can remember, I am american after all! For the longest time I considered burgers to be a pretty simple food. However, one christmas I received a Bob's Burgers cookbook from my parents - and ever since then I have been experimenting making all sorts of wild and delicious burger recipes. However there's still something to be said about a simple no frills burger, and that's exactly what this is.

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground beef
  • Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonnaise
  • Fried egg (optional)

Instructions

  1. Form your ground beef into 4 patties
  2. Cook your patties in a skillet on a stove
    • You want them to be just a little pink
  3. Toast your buns (your burger buns)
  4. Put the patties on the buns and add your toppings
  5. Enjoy!
