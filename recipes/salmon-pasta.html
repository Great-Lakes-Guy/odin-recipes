Quick Creamy Salmon Pasta

Description

A creamy Salmon Pasta recipe that makes for a perfect weeknight meal. This creamy and healthy pasta recipe is easy to make, delicious, and ready in just under 30 minutes.

This Healthy Salmon Pasta recipe comes together in just 30 minutes or less and is a great meal for a busy night that the whole family can enjoy. Serve this pasta dish as a complete meal you can easily swap the salmon for another protein. Loaded with flavor and very nutritious, this salmon pasta will surely satisfy your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 350g farfalle
  • 200g tub light soft cheese
  • squeeze lemon juice
  • 145g pack gravadlax , torn into pieces, or smoked salmon trimmings
  • bunch chives , snipped

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta following pack instructions.
  2. Once it's cooked, drain, reserving some of the water, and tip it back into the pan.
  3. Add the soft cheese, lemon juice and gravadlax or smoked salmon with some pepper and mix well.
  4. Add a little of the pasta water to help form a creamy sauce, and heat through.
  5. Add most of the chives, stir, and divide the pasta mixture between bowls.
  6. Serve with the remaining chives scattered on top.
